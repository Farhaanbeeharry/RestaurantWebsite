Our story

Stéphane Resto is an elegant restaurant of fine cuisine, paying particular attention to the authenticity of its recipes. The ingredients are bought from local farmers, fishermen and food producers. We create beautiful and delicious dishes inspired by the times before, when fresh and natural were not options; they were the norm. But beyond being a culinary collision of ingredients, our dishes are a narrative of our history, our culture and our passion for good food.





Our Specialities

Cooking on hot stone is one of the oldest methods of cooking food.

By cooking on a pre-heated hot stone (in our case marble), you will decide the perfection of your dinner.The natural theater of this method, combined with the purity of taste and health benefits of using minimal oil and no fats, ensures that the dinner will give you an unforgettable experience.

We carefully select all of our ingredients, locally produced, to make sure that they are as close to their origin as possible.
